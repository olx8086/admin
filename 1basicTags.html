Ooty

Officially called Ootacamund, the hill-town is better known by its nickname–Ooty. It is loved by Bollywood directors, honeymooners, families, and even backpackers, for its mountains, lakes, gardens, and waterfalls.

Jog Falls

Jog Falls[6] is a waterfall on the Sharavati river located in Siddapur taluk of Uttara Kannada district[7].
Jog Falls is situated Near Siddapura And Sagara city.
It's view point in Kargal town of Shimoga district, Karnataka, India.

Jog Falls is created by the Sharavati river dropping 253 m (830 ft), making it the third-highest waterfall in India after the Nohkalikai Falls with a drop of 335 m (1,099 ft) in Meghalaya[12] and the Dudhsagar Falls with a drop of 310 m (1,020 ft) in Goa.
